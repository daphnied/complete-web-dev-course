A few facts about our moon

The Moon is an astronomical body that orbits plant Earth, being Earth's only permanaent natural satellite. It is the fifth largest natural satellite in the Solar System, and the largest among planetary satellites relative to the size of the planet that it orbits.

About the Moon

  • Mass: 7.342 x 1022 kg
  • Circumference: 10 921 km
  • Satellite of: The Earth

Facts about the Moon

  1. 4.51 billion years old
  2. The core is solid and iron-rich
  3. Has a permanent asymmetric moon dust cloud
